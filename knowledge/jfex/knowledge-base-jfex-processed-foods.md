Processed Foods EXPO is a zone at JFEX SUMMER 2026 featuring frozen foods, confectionery, health foods, and processed food products from Japan and international exhibitors.

Featured product Frozen Kimbap SPICY KIMBAP from TRUE DELICIOUS CORPORATION. This is authentic Korean kimbap that is frozen and ready to heat in minutes. Available in two varieties: Hokkaido pumpkin spicy oden flavor and kimchi and tuna mayo flavor. Designed for retail stores and food service buyers looking for convenient Korean-Japanese fusion frozen food items for restaurant menus or retail freezer sections.

Featured product Chilled Delicious Soup Hokkaido Pumpkin from Shimizu Foods Co Ltd. This is a rich creamy chilled soup that brings out natural sweetness of Hokkaido pumpkin with the depth and smoothness of fresh cream. A premium ready-to-eat chilled soup product for retail and food service.

This zone is ideal for supermarket frozen food buyers, restaurant chain procurement managers, convenience store category buyers, food service distributors, and wholesalers looking for frozen food innovations, health foods, confectionery, and new processed food products to add to their product lineup.

The Food Collaboration Fair at JFEX is relevant to processed food manufacturers seeking partnerships for product development and collaboration products. The free matching service helps connect buyers with specific processed food exhibitors based on their procurement needs during visitor pre-registration.

The International Food Logistics EXPO Food LogiX is co-located and covers cold chain logistics packaging materials and import export consulting. Cathay Pacific Airways exhibits temperature-controlled air transport for perishables and Kawanishi Warehouse provides cold chain warehousing services. These logistics exhibitors are especially relevant for buyers sourcing frozen food products.
