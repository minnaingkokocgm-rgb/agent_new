Wine and Spirits EXPO is a zone at JFEX SUMMER 2026 featuring wines, whiskies, craft spirits, and non-alcoholic beverages from Japan and international producers. The zone covers everything from craft alcohol to premium wines to innovative non-alcoholic drink products for diverse consumer tastes.

Featured product Satis Dei White Spanish Sauvignon Blanc from Taiheiyo Boeki Co Ltd. This is an affordable yet authentic Spanish white wine with rich fruit aromas. It is an award-winning well-balanced bottle positioned as an accessible premium wine for retail stores and restaurant wine lists. Good for importers looking for mid-tier Spanish wine for the Japanese market.

Featured product Divergence Whisky Otago Pinot Barrel from The Spirits Workshop Limited. This is a New Zealand single malt whisky long-aged in Pinot casks. The flavor profile features sweet malt intertwined with fruit notes and gentle oak. It represents the growing craft whisky movement and is a distinctive product not offered by major Scottish distilleries. Good for bars, specialty liquor stores, and importers looking for unique craft whisky.

Featured in the co-located Japanese Food Export EXPO is CRAFT LIQUEUR Kumano Gin Umeshu and Whisky-Finished Umeshu from MK Trading. These are innovative Japanese craft liqueurs blending traditional umeshu with gin and whisky finishing for international export markets.

This zone is ideal for wine importers, liquor store buyers, restaurant sommeliers, hotel beverage directors, bar owners, and alcohol distributors looking for new wines, craft spirits, Japanese whisky alternatives, and non-alcoholic beverage products.

The JFEX AWARDS include a category for overseas products entering Japan for the first time which wine and spirits exhibitors may compete in. The free matching service helps buyers find specific wine and spirits exhibitors matching their procurement requirements.
