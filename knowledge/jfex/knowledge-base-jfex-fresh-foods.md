Fresh Foods EXPO is a zone at JFEX SUMMER 2026 where buyers compare fresh ingredients across categories: agricultural products, livestock products, and marine products. Exhibitors showcase fresh produce, meats, seafood, and minimally processed fresh food items from Japan and international suppliers.

Featured product Whole-Pressed Mandarin Orange Juice from Neighborhood Co Ltd. This is 100 percent straight juice made from whole-pressed mandarin oranges with no additives or concentrates. Features a pulpy texture with natural sweetness and tartness. A premium fresh juice product appealing to health-conscious consumers and premium retail channels.

Featured in the co-located Japanese Food Export EXPO is Wagyu Beef from Taiheiyo Boeki Co Ltd. Premium Japanese Wagyu beef with strong international demand especially in Asian and Western markets. Also featured is Japanese Domestic Organic Matcha from Chiran Tea Garden Co Ltd, a high-demand fresh green tea ingredient for both domestic premium retail and international export.

This zone is ideal for supermarket fresh food buyers, restaurant chefs and procurement managers, hotel food and beverage directors, fresh produce importers and wholesalers, food service distributors, and meal kit companies sourcing fresh ingredients.

The International Food Logistics EXPO Food LogiX is especially important for fresh food buyers because it covers cold chain logistics. Cathay Pacific Airways exhibits temperature-controlled air transport specialized for perishables with certified management systems serving approximately 100 cities worldwide from Japan. Kawanishi Warehouse provides cold chain warehousing and handles export customs clearance and various inspections including animal quarantine. New Japan Certification Association offers import and export food testing analysis and regulatory compliance consulting for fresh products entering different country markets.

The free matching service helps connect buyers with specific fresh food suppliers based on procurement needs including seasonal availability and import and export requirements. The JFEX AWARDS are decided by visitor votes and include categories for fresh food products.
