JFEX SUMMER 2026 is the 8th International Food & Beverage Business Week at Tokyo Big Sight. Dates are June 24 to June 26, 2026, open 10:00 to 17:00 daily. Over 700 companies exhibit more than 6,500 products from 20+ countries and regions. The organizer is RX Japan, Japan's largest exhibition organizer. JFEX SUMMER is a B2B trade show for buyers from retail, wholesale, trading companies, and restaurant management. General consumers should not attend.

The exhibition is composed of 5 specialized zones: Processed Foods EXPO featuring frozen foods and confectionery, Wine & Spirits EXPO with craft alcohol and non-alcoholic beverages, Premium Foods EXPO with high-grade gourmet foods and gift items, Fresh Foods EXPO with agricultural livestock and marine products, and Inbound Foods EXPO with souvenirs and halal foods for international visitors.

Key value proposition: discover unique compelling products for product differentiation, private brand development, and finding new suppliers. JFEX helps buyers find products they won't find anywhere else.

Visitor registration is free until Friday June 19, 2026 at 18:00 JST. After the deadline, admission costs ¥5,000 including tax. There are two registration types: VIP registration for buyers and purchasing decision-makers at manager level and above which includes lounge access, and general registration for all other visitors. After registering you receive a confirmation email with badge data to color-print and bring for direct entry. On-site printing is available at the venue using the QR code from the email. For registration help call 048-233-9478.

If you don't have a company name enter Sole Proprietor or Individual Farmer in the company field. For registration problems check that all required fields are filled and you haven't selected multiple options in single-choice fields. If your email says already registered but you can't find the confirmation email check your spam or junk mail folder for a message with subject Visitor registration is complete. To register multiple people send the form URL to each person for individual registration. Contact phone is 048-233-9478 and email is jfex.jp@rxglobal.com.

Tokyo Big Sight is at 3-11-1 Ariake Koto-ku Tokyo 135-0063. Nearest stations: Kokusai-Tenjijo Station on the Rinkai Line approximately 7 minutes walk away, and Tokyo Big Sight Station on the Yurikamome Line approximately 3 minutes walk away.

Five key visitor points. Point 1 exclusive products many making their Japan debut. The Food Collaboration Fair is a dedicated business matching area for product development and collaboration product creation with manufacturers skilled in collaboration products.
