Inbound Foods EXPO is a zone at JFEX SUMMER 2026 designed for the growing inbound tourism demand in Japan. This zone features souvenirs, halal foods, and products specifically developed for international visitors to Japan. Exhibitors present food products that appeal to tourists, international travelers, and the increasingly diverse consumer base in Japan.

Product categories in this zone include Japanese souvenir foods and gift items popular with tourists, halal-certified food products for Muslim visitors, products with multilingual packaging for international appeal, and regional specialty foods positioned as travel souvenirs. The Tenkyu Suiu Junmai Daiginjo premium sake from EBS Trade Co Ltd featured in the co-located Japanese Food Export EXPO is an example of a premium Japanese product suitable for inbound tourist markets.

The inbound tourism market in Japan is growing rapidly. With international visitor numbers increasing, demand for tourist-oriented food products, halal-certified items, and culturally appropriate souvenirs is expanding significantly. This zone helps buyers source products specifically for this market.

This zone is ideal for airport and duty-free shop buyers, tourist destination retailers, hotel gift shop buyers, halal food importers and distributors, souvenir wholesalers, travel retail operators, and companies serving Japan's growing international resident and visitor populations.

The Food Collaboration Fair at JFEX provides opportunities for product development specifically targeting inbound tourist markets by combining Japanese food culture with international preferences and packaging requirements.

Lifestyle Week TOKYO is co-located and accessible with the same badge featuring non-food lifestyle products and souvenirs that complement food souvenirs for the inbound tourism market. The Japanese Food Export EXPO features additional Japanese products popular with international visitors and suitable for export markets. The free matching service helps connect buyers with exhibitors specializing in halal foods, souvenir products, and items for international visitors.
