## 8. FAQ

### Q1: I don't have a company name — what should I do if I can't register?
Farmers and others who are not affiliated with a company can still attend.
Please enter "Sole Proprietor," "Individual Farmer," or similar in the company name field, and enter "None" for the job title and department fields.
For the address, please enter an address where you can receive mail from the organizer.
> This exhibition is a B2B business meeting event. Please refrain from attending if you are a general consumer.

### Q2: I click the "Next" button but the screen doesn't advance
First, please check whether all required fields are filled in, and whether you have selected multiple options where only one should be chosen.
The most common cause of registration not progressing is the above.
If it still doesn't work after entering correctly, please contact TEL: 048-233-9478.

### Q3: It says "The email address entered is already registered" but I can't find the registration completion email
The visitor registration completion email may be in your spam/junk mail folder.
Please search your mailbox again with the subject line "Visitor registration is complete."
If you still cannot find it, please contact TEL: 048-233-9478.

### Q4: I want to register multiple people, but I don't know the email addresses of those accompanying me
Please send the form URL to the person accompanying you and ask them to complete their own visitor registration.
